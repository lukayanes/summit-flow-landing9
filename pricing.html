Questions & answers.

Is Summit Flow only for wholesalers?

No — it’s built for wholesalers, but it works for any business that needs automation when new leads come in (realtors, property managers, home repair, contractors, and more).

What happens when a new lead comes in?

Summit Flow can instantly text/email the lead, notify your team, and place them into a pipeline so nothing falls through the cracks.

Can this replace my spreadsheets and scattered notes?

Yes — contacts, notes, tasks, follow-ups, and pipeline stages are centralized so you can track every conversation and next step.

Can I use this for multiple services or business types?

Absolutely. You can run different pipelines and follow-up sequences for things like acquisitions, dispositions, property management inquiries, repairs, or general sales leads.

Do you help with setup and importing my existing leads?

Yes. We’ll help you get your pipeline built, connect your lead sources, and import contacts so you can start converting leads fast.

Will this work with my lead sources (forms, ads, calls)?

In most cases, yes. Summit Flow can connect to common lead sources like web forms, ads, inbound calls, and manual entries, then trigger automations automatically.
